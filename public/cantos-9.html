Circle 9:

Treachery

The deepest, coldest part of Hell. Treachery is seen as the ultimate sin because it violates a bond of trust. Unlike the fire of the upper circles, this is a frozen lake (Cocytus).

Caina

Traitors to kin (frozen up to their necks).

Antenora

Traitors to country.

Ptolomea

Traitors to guests (their tears freeze in their eye sockets).

Judecca

Traitors to lords/benefactors (completely submerged in ice).

LUCIFER

At the very center of the Earth stands Lucifer. He has three faces and eternally chews on the three greatest traitors of history: Judas Iscariot, Brutus, and Cassius.
