Descent

Circle 2:
Lust

As Dante moves deeper, the true "shrieks" of Hell begin. Here, the judge Minos sits at the entrance, wrapping his tail around himself to signal which circle a soul must go to.

The Sin

Those who placed their sexual appetites above their reason.

The Punishment

Howling Gale

The souls are swept up in a terrible, howling gale that never stops. Just as they let their passions blow them about aimlessly on Earth, they are now tossed and smashed against jagged rocks by an eternal wind, never able to touch the ground or find rest.
