CIRCLE 7: VIOLENCE

Against Neighbors

We have crossed into the Seventh Circle: Violence. The first ring holds those violent against their neighbors.

Murderers, warmongers, and tyrants are plunged into the Phlegethon... a vast, churning river of boiling blood, their depth determined by the severity of their guilt.

RING II

Against Themselves

The second ring is entirely different. It is a dead, twisted forest holding those who committed violence against themselves. The suicides are trapped as gnarled, blackened trees with poisonous thorns.

They have no voice... they can only speak their eternal pain when their brittle branches are snapped and bleed dark sap.

RING III

Against God & Nature

Finally, the third ring: a sterile, desolate wasteland for those violent against God and Nature. The blasphemers, sodomites, and usurers are trapped on a sprawling desert of white-hot, burning sand.

And from the pitch-black sky above... broad flakes of fire fall slowly, like burning snow, eternally scorching them from all sides.
