01 // The Setup

FAIRGIG: The End of the "Pay-to-Play" Lie

Every gigging musician knows the feeling.

It’s 2:00 AM. Your ears are ringing, your gear is loaded in the van, and your shirt is soaked with sweat. You packed the room tonight. You did your job.

Then comes the walk to the back office to get paid.

02 // The Reality Check

Suddenly, the promoter’s math gets fuzzy. The door take is mysteriously low. A sudden $200 "sound engineer fee" appears out of nowhere. A "promotional cut" is deducted.

You’re handed a wad of small bills that doesn't even cover your drummer's gas money. You don't have the data to fight back, so you take the money, swallow your pride, and drive home.

The live music industry calls this "paying your dues."

We call it what it is:

THEFT.

03 // The Catalyst

For decades, the local music economy has been built on a foundation of blind hope and opaque handshakes. Platforms like Bandsintown and Eventbrite only care about getting fans in the door.

No one is protecting the artist when the door closes. Venues hold all the data, which means they hold all the power.

FairGig was built to burn that power dynamic to the ground.

We didn't build this because we saw a "gap in the SaaS market." We built this because we’ve lived it. FairGig’s founder, Cliff Franco, has spent 15 years fronting bands in the trenches—from punk and alternative rock to reggae and blues. He spent over a decade dealing with the bait-and-switch deals that kill independent artists' careers before they even start.

04 // The Data Layer

FairGig is the weapon artists have been waiting for.

It is the financial truth layer of live music.

We are replacing the shady, verbal, back-alley deals with undeniable, mathematical reality. Through our Show Economics Simulator, artists can test a venue's deal before they ever plug in an amp.

What happens if 50 people show up?
You see the exact math.
What happens if we sell it out?
You see your exact cut.
What are the hidden venue fees?
They are exposed, locked in, and tracked.

05 // The Truth Tax

By taking the guesswork out of the gig, FairGig turns an artist's draw into pure leverage. We replace blind faith with hard data, and we replace handshake disputes with an unchangeable audit trail.

But we aren't stopping at software. We are introducing a Truth Tax to the industry. By arming an army of musicians with this tool, we are forcing venues into the light.

Good venues and honest promoters who pay fairly will be verified, celebrated, and rewarded with the best talent. And the shady promoters who refuse to use FairGig? Their silence will be the ultimate red flag. Artists will know exactly who to avoid.

We are done hoping for fair treatment. We are doing the math. We are bringing transparency to an industry that has hidden in the dark for too long.

Before, you guessed. Now, you know. Welcome to FairGig.
