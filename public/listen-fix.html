Observed Symptom

Root Cause
Bearing wear causing radial play beyond tolerance
Skill Level
Intermediate · ~45 min estimated
Repair Steps (Intermediate)
1
Lock-Out / Tag-Out. De-energize the generator and affix LOTO tags before any contact with the turbo housing.
2
Secondary Seal Extraction. Remove the M12 retaining bolts (torque: 42 Nm) using a 19mm deep socket. Do not reuse the crush washers.
3
Impeller Inspection. Measure radial play with a dial indicator. Replace if deflection exceeds 0.05mm per factory spec.
4
Reassemble & Verify. Torque M12 bolts to 42 Nm in a star pattern. Run at idle for 5 minutes; confirm whine is absent at 3,000 RPM.
