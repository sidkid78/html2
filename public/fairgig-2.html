The Arsenal

Weapons Against Exploitation

We built FairGig because "exposure" doesn't pay for gas, strings, or rent. Artists have operated at an informational disadvantage for decades. We are giving the leverage back to the creators. Here are the tools systematically leveling the playing field.

02
Tool 01

Show Economics Simulator

Never sign blind again. Input the venue’s proposed deal—door split, guarantee, ticket price, estimated draw. The simulator generates an instantaneous projection of who makes exactly what at every capacity level.

Break-Even Analysis
Know exactly how many tickets you must sell just to cover the venue's "overhead" fees.
Hidden Fee Detection
Flags suspicious or predatory production costs compared to historical city averages.
03
Tool 02

The Settlement Ledger

The days of getting handed crumpled cash and confusing math on a cocktail napkin are over. The Settlement Ledger tracks real-time box office numbers and applies agreed-upon terms immutably.

Transparent Math
Both artist and venue view the same interface, seeing payout models update live.
Instant Disbursements
Automated split payments directly to band members' bank accounts the moment the show clears.
04
Tool 03

Reputation Profiles

This is where the Truth Tax is enforced. Every gig booked and settled through FairGig builds a permanent public profile. Bad actors get exposed; ethical venues attract the best talent.

Venue Fairness Score
A composite 0-100 score based on historic payout reliability and artist reviews.
Artist Promotor Metric
Help venues trust you by demonstrating an verified track record of high draw and professionalism.
