01
Listen & Fix: The Antidote to Mechanical Anxiety

The Problem:
The Sound of Dread

You’re driving down the highway when you hear it: a rhythmic thunk-rattle-hiss coming from under the hood. Your stomach drops. Or maybe it’s the high-pitched squeal of your washing machine right before it dies, leaving you with a basement full of water.

In that moment, you aren't thinking about machines. You’re feeling a deep, primal anxiety. What is that? Am I in danger? Am I about to get ripped off for $2,000 by a mechanic?

Modern machinery has become a terrifying black box. We’ve lost the power to understand the things we rely on every single day.

02

Shazam for the
Physical World

We built Listen & Fix because we believe no one should be held hostage by a broken machine. We believe in the right to repair, the triumph of fixing it yourself, and the peace of mind that comes from knowing exactly what’s wrong.

Imagine holding your phone up to a sputtering engine, a rattling HVAC unit, or a leaky pipe. The app simply listens and watches. Within seconds, it doesn't just tell you the problem—it hands you the solution.

03

How We Give You
Superpowers

We’ve taken the most advanced AI in the world (Google Gemini) and given it eyes, ears, and the brain of a master mechanic.

  • It Hears What You Can’t Whether it’s a microscopic high-frequency bearing whine or a sluggish engine knock, our AI acoustic engine diagnoses the exact mechanical failure just by listening.
  • It Reads Every Manual Ever Written The moment it identifies your machine, our system instantly hunts down, reads, and memorizes the exact service manuals, technical bulletins, and torque specs for your specific model.
04
The Interface

The Digital Foreman

You are never left alone. Our interface is designed to be the calmest voice in the room. No cluttered tech-jargon—just a clean, authoritative, step-by-step guide.

It tells you what tools you need, how dangerous the fix is, and even finds the exact parts at your local auto-parts or hardware store.

05
The Vision

Empowering the
Everyday Fixer

Listen & Fix isn't just an app; it's an ecosystem designed to turn helpless consumers into capable do-it-yourselfers, and everyday technicians into master diagnosticians.

We are stripping away the guesswork. We are taking the fear out of the flashing "Check Engine" light. We are putting the power, the knowledge, and the wrench back into your hands.

Listen. Diagnose. Fix.
